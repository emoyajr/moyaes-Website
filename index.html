About Me

Hi! I'm Edgar, pursuing a B.S. in Applied Mathematics at UC Merced with an expected graduation in Spring 2027.

I thrive at the intersection of systems architecture and algorithmic optimization. While my journey began with the precision of Numerical Analysis, I quickly realized that elegant models are only as effective as the data feeding them. This insight drove me to transition into Data Engineering, where I now focus on constructing reliable, high-volume data pipelines.

My goal is to ensure that scientific and business models aren't just theoretically sound, but are fueled by high-quality, real-time data at scale.
