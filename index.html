About Me

Hi! I'm Edgar, an undergraduate student at the University of California, Merced, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics with an emphasis in Computational and Data Sciences. Expected graduation: Spring 2027.

My interests lie at the intersection of data engineering, numerical analysis, and systems optimization. I'm passionate about building reliable data infrastructure that supports real-world applications, from ETL pipelines and automated monitoring systems to ensuring data quality at scale.

My goal is to bridge the gap between mathematical theory and practical data systems, ensuring that models and insights are powered by solid, dependable infrastructure.
